About user researchers

User researchers help ensure that digital services are build on solid user centred principles. 

They work with users to understand what motivates them to use a service.   They develop insight into user frustrations and pain with existing services.  This helps  future designs to address them and make user experience better. 

They help understand where users need support to complete their service journey ('assisted digital'). 

They work with design colleagues to reflect the insight gained in the service design.  They then take those designs and share them with users. 

They test designs with users and gathering their feedback. This helps to iterate the service, improving each time. 

Throughout, user researchers help ensure services reflects user needs.
